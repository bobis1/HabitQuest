# HabitQuest
Making maintaining habits fun!
HabitQuest is a gameified habit tracker with a fun-functional boss fight to motivate players.
## The Problem
Most habit trackers are bad at motivating users, often letting them let go of their habits due to poor engagement.
## The Solution
We decided to add a twist to our habit tracker. In addition with our modern tracking system, we have a special boss fight that you can fight with your good habits and broken bad habits.
